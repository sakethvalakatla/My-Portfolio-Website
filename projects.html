    Digital Well-being Platform - Debug Mental Health

    Debug Mental Health is a web platform developed as a submission for the
    CodeFury 6.0 Hackathon , Developed in under 10 hours.
    Team Members: Saketh V, SaiUttej R, Supradeep R, Akshay R, PavanKumar S
    Users can create accounts, write personal journals, engage with the community
    journal, play relaxing games, listen to calming music, access mental health resources,
    and find inspiration through a curated collection of quotes.
    We integrated external APIs such as Quotable for quotes and Spotify for music.
    Built with React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Firebase for backend functionality.
